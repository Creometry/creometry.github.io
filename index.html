Overview

What is Creometry?

Creometry is a managed application platform leverageing a modern architecture (known as “serverless”) and open-source technologies (i.e. kubernetes, Linux containers) to deliver a developer friendly experience and reduce the cost of hosting software. The platform abstracts infrastructure completely, allowing developers to focus on business logic and delivering applications faster. In addition to the its container-as-a-service platform, Creometry creates an ecosystem consisting of a training academy for individuals looking to add new technological skills, and a startup lab to accompany the most promising digital business ideas.

Creometry offers support to organizations planning to migrate to a container-based deployment for increased agility, optimal resource utilization and higher quality of service.
